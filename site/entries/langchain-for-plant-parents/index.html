§ 2.0 capabilities
F-01

Composable care chains

Wire watering, misting, rotation, and fertilizing into a single pipeline. When a step fails, Tendril retries with exponential backoff before paging you at 3am about a succulent that needed nothing.

F-02

Per-plant vector memory

Every leaf, droop, and skipped watering gets embedded and stored. Your pothos remembers what you did in March. Retrieval-augmented guilt, available on every query.

F-03

Leaf-level observability

Spans, traces, and p99 latency for photosynthesis. Find the exact step that killed the basil, roughly six hours after the basil already knew.
