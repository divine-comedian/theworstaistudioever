4.17 / core systems

The Dawn Circle

Our proprietary sunrise engine shrinks the playable night in real time. Players caught in daylight are rotated gently into sleep and removed from the match. Ranked dawns begin at 5:58 AM sharp and are not negotiable, because balance.

4.17 / crafting

Excuse Crafting

Harvest raw materials from your immediate environment: a podcast, a wiki rabbit hole, a group chat that just got interesting. Combine them into load-bearing structures that keep you up well past everything you promised yourself. Crafted excuses are visible to other players, which we consider a feature.

4.17 / progression

Sleep Debt Battle Pass

One hundred tiers of exclusive cosmetics for an avatar nobody sees because it is dark. Progress accrues exclusively between 1 AM and 5 AM local time. Yes, local time. We know where you are.
