The Service

Three instruments, each addressed to you personally.

Instrument No. 1

Legacy Rates

Send money at the exchange rate from the year you emigrated. The difference accrues quietly and is invoiced to your estate, addressed to whichever child answers.

Instrument No. 2

Grandchild Rails

Recurring birthday transfers with a handwriting-simulated memo line. Names, ages, and preferred currencies tracked on your behalf, so the affection is fully automated.

Instrument No. 3

Tab Consolidation

We merge your open tabs at up to four expat bars into one monthly statement, denominated in whichever currency performed best. For you or for us, the statement doesn't say.
