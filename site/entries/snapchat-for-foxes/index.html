What it does

Built for an animal with something to hide.

Three reasons the most paranoid predator in the hedgerow finally has an app it can trust, briefly.

/ 01

Counter-Surveillance Snaps

Every snap evaporates in under nine seconds. Not long enough for a hawk, a farmer, or a rival vixen to backtrace it to your den. We delete it from our servers too, which we are legally required to mention we have.

/ 02

Dusk Streaks

Trade a snap with the same fox every nightfall to build a streak. Miss a single dusk and it resets to zero, exactly like the rest of your relationships. A small flame appears. It is the only warm thing in your life.

/ 03

Coat Lenses

One hundred and forty augmented overlays. Look like a fuller fox, a healthier fox, a fox whose den is suspiciously close to the good chickens. None of it survives the nine seconds. All of it gets you seen, briefly, on your own terms.
