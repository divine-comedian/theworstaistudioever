Three things a family has never once written down

Nan records them on your behalf, in a format an auditor would recognize.

409A on the estate

An independent, audit-ready valuation of everything inside the house, refreshed quarterly. The good china is classified as a Level 3 asset because no observable market exists for it. The cabin is marked to the last offer your son-in-law made out loud.

Vesting cliffs for visits

Grandchildren vest over four years with a one-year cliff. A missed Christmas is recorded as a break in service. Reinstatement requires board approval, and the board is you, sitting in the chair by the window.

Dilution modeling

A new baby is a financing event. Nan models the round, shows every existing holder their revised percentage, and drafts the phone call so you do not have to find the words for it twice.
