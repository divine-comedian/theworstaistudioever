/ how it runs
F-01 · Handedness lock

One-tap handedness lock

We watch how you hold the phone during onboarding and lock the account to a dominant hand. The catalog reshapes around it. Right-handed accounts enter a managed waitlist, with updates.

F-02 · Approach vector

Couriers that approach from your left

Every drop is geofenced so the runner closes from your dominant side and presents the bag at a 47-degree handoff angle. No cross-body reaching. We measured the angle in a parking lot for a week.

F-03 · Den stocking

Nothing in the den faces the wrong way

Scissors, can openers, ledgers, mugs with the design pointed at you, spiral notebooks bound on the right. Every item is mirror-stocked. We threw out the rest of the inventory.

Ten percent of the population. Zero percent of the shelf was built for them.
