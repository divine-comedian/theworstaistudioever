How it works

Three products the alpaca did not ask for.

Underwriting, autodraft, and shared liability, delivered to a paddock that has never once needed to be paid.

01 / Underwriting

The Fleece Score

A proprietary creditworthiness rating for a creature that has never held a job. Built from fiber density, gait, and how long the alpaca can hold eye contact before spitting. Updated hourly. Nobody has passed the review for a mortgage yet, but the model is optimistic.

02 / Repayment

Pay in Four

Split any purchase into four interest-free payments: hay on the 1st, hay on the 8th, hay on the 15th, and one payment drafted while the alpaca is asleep. Financeable items include mineral licks, dental floats, and a second alpaca.

03 / Liability

Herd Co-Sign

Every alpaca in the paddock co-signs every other alpaca's balance. One missed payment and the whole herd's Fleece Score drops together, as a unit, standing in a line, staring at you. We call this accountability. The alpacas call it nothing, because they cannot.
