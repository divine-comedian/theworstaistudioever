Motion-Triggered Uploads

Skulk starts recording the moment a fox enters frame and publishes before the fox has left the garden. Foxes cannot operate a record button. We have classified this as a feature and moved on.

Scream Recognition

Our audio model sorts all 28 documented fox screams by emotional register, from territorial to inconsolable, and tags every clip automatically. Confidence stays high as long as no fox is actually screaming nearby.
