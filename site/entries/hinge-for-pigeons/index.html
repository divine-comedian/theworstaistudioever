How the matching works

Three systems, one gutter.

Every pairing runs through the same pipeline a serious relationship platform would use, pointed at a bird that already mates for life and did not ask for any of this.

01 / verification

Coo Verification

Every profile is authenticated by a three-second coo sample. No doves posing as pigeons, no gulls in from the coast. If the vocalization doesn't clear our reference range, the account never goes live.

02 / geography

Homing Radius Matching

We only surface birds inside your natural return-flight range, so an evening out never means abandoning the route that gets you home before dark. Distance is measured in wingbeats, not miles.

03 / scoring

Nest-Readiness Score

A 1-to-10 rating of how prepared each bird is to commit to a shared gutter, weighted on twig-gathering history, prior ledge tenure, and how they behave around a dropped bagel. Updated hourly.
