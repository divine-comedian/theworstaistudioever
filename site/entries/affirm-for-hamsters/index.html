How Hoard works

Underwriting built around how a hamster actually lives.

Three models run on every account. None of them have been explained to the borrower.

Cheek-pouch underwriting

We set credit limits by measuring how much your hamster can physically carry at once. Two full pouches, two thousand dollars. The model is non-negotiable and faintly impressed.

Your live HoardScore

A creditworthiness score from 0 to 850 that rewards wheel mileage and penalizes naps. Most hamsters sleep through their best rate, and we are not able to wake them on their behalf.

Lifespan amortization

Every purchase spreads across your hamster's projected remaining months, recalculated each night you confirm the borrower is still with us. Miss a confirmation and the balance accelerates.
