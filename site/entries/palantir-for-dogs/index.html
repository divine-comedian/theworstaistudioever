01Capabilities

Three competencies. One ground truth.

Houndsight does not produce dashboards. Houndsight produces decisions. Each capability ships with a model card, an audit trail, and a designated point of contact at our office in Tysons Corner.

01.01

Behavioral Pattern Fusion

Cross-references fourteen sensor vendors (GPS, weight pads, microphone arrays, kibble-bowl IMUs) to determine whether your dog is operating within its established personality envelope. Excursions trigger a priority-three alert.

Latency · 92msModel · BPF-7
01.02

Adversarial Squirrel Modeling

A continuously-updated graph database of every squirrel observed within 500m of the household, weighted by historical engagement frequency. Every walk ships with an Escalation Likelihood score and three pre-cleared response options.

Coverage · 500mModel · ASM-3
01.03

Pack Sentiment Forecasting

Natural-language ingestion of every “good boy” uttered in the home, attributed to the correct subject via on-device speaker diarization. Output is a weekly Affection Allocation Report, stratified by dog, with statistically significant deviations flagged.

Cadence · WeeklyModel · PSF-2.1
