Loot Appraisal Engine

A vision model grades every object at the curb on the standard rarity scale. A cracked rice cooker pings Uncommon. An untouched Eames knockoff pings Epic. A mattress is Bind-on-Pickup and cannot be traded, mailed, or explained to your partner.

Bulk-trash night is endgame content. Assemble a raid, assign roles, and pull on a shared timer. The tank holds the lid. Healers handle tetanus risk. If someone ninjas the credenza, that is a guild matter, not a Haul matter.
